Installing Palettes

1. Place the downloaded palette image into the "Rain World/Assets/Futile/Resources/Palettes" folder.
2. Rename it to follow the naming convention of the other palettes, ie: palette36.png, palette37.png, etc.
3. Use Dev Tools to apply the palette to your map(s).
