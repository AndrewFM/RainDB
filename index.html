RainDB, for short.

Welcome to RainDB! This website will be undergoing revamps with the release of Rain World: Downpour.

  • If you are looking to re-install old versions of Rain World and play with previously released mods, visit the Legacy Mods page.
  • If you are looking to play with mods in the current (v1.9.XX) versions of Rain World and Downpour, visit Rain World's Steam Workshop for now.
  • If you are looking for resources for creating your own Rain World mods, visit the Rain World Modding Wiki and the Modding Tools page!
