Rain World Mod Database

RainDB, for short.

All mods are for Rain World v1.01. Want your mod to be here? Check out the Submit page!

More Slugcats (v1.3)

By AndrewFM
Mod Type: Slugcat Abilities

Introduces five new playable slugcats into Rain World, each with their own unique playstyles, abilities, pros and cons.

Buffed Lizards

By Combo Pyro
Mod Type: Creature Edits

Changes the attributes and abilities of all of the lizards in the game, generally making them tougher and more intimidating.

Side House

By AndrewFM
Mod Type: New Region

Adds an entirely new region to Rain World that connects Shoreline, Five Pebbles, and the Depths. It's an amalgamation of many of the map files that went unused in the final game.

Hurricane Mod (v0.2.1)

By Mikronaut
Mod Type: Difficulty Edit

An ultra hard difficulty mode. Less shelters, stricter requirements, and much fiercer creatures and environments.

Alien World: Outskirts

By Grimmitar
Mod Type: Region Revamp

A revamp for the Outskirts, making the world feel more strange and alien. Come across neurons and leap through the air in zero gravity, calling for precise jumps and tricky gameplay.

Custom Colors

By Beau
Mod Type: Utility

Allows you to customize your slugcat's color! Finally you can roam the industrial wastes as a magnificent colored creature.

Spear Rifle

By Beau
Mod Type: Object Edits

Adds an rapid-fire automatic rifle to the game that shoots out spears!

Shoreline Revamp

By BushHidden
Mod Type: Region Revamp

Adds big waves, fog, darkness and other cool things to the Shoreline region to make it more visually appealing.

Multiplayer Mode

By Omega

Enables a partially functional, early access version of the multiplayer mode that will be released with the next Rain World update.

Grapecat

By josbird
Mod Type: Slugcat Abilities

Turns Slugcat into Grapecat. Grapecat is strong and can hold two spears at once. He is also a talented spear-swallower, though his methods are a little unorthodox. Plus, he is purple, because purple is the best color.

Moon's Waterproof Shelter

By Six Grains Of Slugcat
Mod Type: Region Revamp

Makes Moon's room in Shoreline rain-proof and flood-proof, adjusts the palette and adds some new items to make the room more detailed, lively and vibrant. Also Moon needs to be safe because she's the softest being and should be protected.
