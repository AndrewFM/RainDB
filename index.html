Mods

These aren't all the mods you can get for your game, but here's some of them!
Want your mod to be here? Check out the Upload page!

Last updated on Friday, 2nd of June, 2017.

Grapecat

By josbird
Mod Type: .dll
Rain World Version: v1.01

Turns Slugcat into Grapecat. Grapecat is strong and can hold two spears at once. He is also a talented spear-swallower, though his methods are a little unorthodox. Plus, he is purple, because purple is the best color.

DaddyComplex

By BushHidden
Mod Type: Region Revamp
Rain World Version: v1.01

The DaddyComplex mod is part of the "Daddy World Project" which aims to create a total conversion of the game, in which every area is infested with Daddy Long Legs. This mod converts the Industrial Complex region.

Moon's Waterproof Shelter

By Six Grains Of Slugcat
Mod Type: Region Revamp
Rain World Version: v1.01

Makes Moon's room in Shoreline rain-proof and flood-proof, adjusts the palette and adds some new items to make the room more detailed, lively and vibrant. Also Moon needs to be safe because she's the softest being and should be protected.

Literally Unplayable

By josbird
Mod Type: .dll
Rain World Version: v1.01

Cycles are now 10% as long. Godspeed you magnificent bastard.

Spear Rifle

By Beau
Mod Type: .dll
Rain World Version: v1.01

The Spear Rifle Mod adds an automatic spear rifle to the starting chamber of the game. Alternatively, the Spear Rifle Rock Replacer replaces every rock in the game with a spear rifle if you don't feel like starting a new game.

Custom Colors

By Beau
Mod Type: .dll
Rain World Version: v1.01

Allows you to customize your slugcat's color! Finally you can roam the industrial wastes as a magnificent colored creature.

Farm Arrays V2

Mod Type: Region Revamp
Rain World Version: v1.01

Part of a mod series called "Rain World V2" which adds, among other effects, Lightburn and Bloom to specific regions to make them more visually appealing. This one edits the Farm Arrays region.

Side House

By AndrewFM
Mod Type: New Region
Rain World Version: v1.01

This mod adds an entirely new region to Rain World, called Side House, that connects between Shoreline, Five Pebbles, and the Depths. The region is an amalgamation of many of the map files in the game data that went unused in the final game.

Alien World: Outskirts

By Grimmitar
Mod Type: Region Revamp
Rain World Version: v1.01

Alien World is a collection of revamps for old and uninteresting regions in Rain World. This revamp is for the Outskirts, the starting area of the game. Come across neurons and leap through the air in zero gravity, calling for precise jumps and tricky gamplay.
