Mods

These are the mods you can get for your game. Check them out!

Grapecat Mod

By josbird
Rain World Version: v1.01

Turns Slugcat into Grapecat. Grapecat is strong and can hold two spears at once. He is also a talented spear-swallower, though his methods are a little unorthodox. Plus, he is purple, because purple is the best color.

DaddyComplex Mod

By BushHidden
Rain World Version: v1.01

The DaddyComplex mod is part of the "Daddy World Project" which aims to create a total conversion of the game, in which every area is infested with Daddy Long Legs. This mod converts the Industrial Complex region.

Moon's Waterproof Shelter

By Six Grains
Rain World Version: v1.01

Makes Moon's room in Shoreline rain-proof and flood-proof, adjusts the palette and adds some new items to make the room more detailed, lively and vibrant. Also Moon needs to be safe because she's the softest being and should be protected.
